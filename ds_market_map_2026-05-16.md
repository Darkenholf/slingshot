# DS Market Map for California Big-Tech Search

Date: 2026-05-16

## Classification Logic

I classify DS roles by the decision surface they own, not by job title. Titles are noisy: "Data Scientist", "Product Data Scientist", "Research Data Scientist", "Business Data Scientist", and "Scientist" can overlap. The useful split for preparation is:

1. Product / Experimentation DS: owns product metrics, funnels, A/B tests, causal inference, roadmap recommendations.
2. Ads / Measurement / Marketing Science DS: owns incrementality, attribution, ads delivery, bidding/auction diagnostics, advertiser ROI.
3. Marketplace / Pricing / Optimization DS: owns two-sided or multi-sided marketplace health, pricing, matching, supply-demand, incentives, LTV.
4. Payments / FinTech / Risk / Fraud DS: owns checkout, payment success, fraud/abuse, risk, credit, financial products, loss reduction.
5. ML / Search / Recommendation / Evaluation DS: owns model quality, ranking/search evaluation, AI product metrics, offline/online evaluation, model diagnostics.
6. Strategic / Finance / GTM / Business DS: owns forecasting, segmentation, unit economics, pricing strategy, sales/marketing analytics.
7. Operations / People / Support / Insurance DS: owns internal operations, workforce, support, reliability, insurance costs, diagnostics.

## Preparation Tracks

### Track A: Product / Experimentation DS
Target companies: Meta, Google, Apple Services, DoorDash, Uber Identity, Snap, LinkedIn, Salesforce, Adobe.
Resume emphasis: metrics definition, funnel analysis, A/B testing, causal inference, stakeholder recommendations, product impact.
Interview prep: product sense, experiment design, SQL, metric debugging, executive communication.

### Track B: Marketplace / Pricing / Optimization DS
Target companies: Uber, Airbnb, DoorDash, Instacart-like teams, Google Fi/Pricing, Stripe growth.
Resume emphasis: optimization, demand/supply dynamics, pricing models, elasticity, LTV, forecasting, decision-making under uncertainty.
Interview prep: marketplace case studies, causal inference, experimentation with interference, time-series/forecasting, optimization tradeoffs.

### Track C: Payments / Risk / Fraud / FinTech DS
Target companies: Stripe, PayPal, Block/Cash App, OpenAI Financial Engineering, Uber Identity/Insurance, DoorDash Integrity.
Resume emphasis: risk models, anomaly detection, financial systems, fraud tradeoffs, loss/cost reduction, precision/recall, calibration, production monitoring.
Interview prep: classification metrics, rare events, fraud economics, payment funnel, causal impact of mitigations, SQL/Python.

### Track D: ML / Search / Evaluation DS
Target companies: Google Search/Applied AI, Apple AIML/Maps/Camera, Microsoft AI, OpenAI Product/Codex/Safety, Airbnb Platform, Walmart Search-like roles.
Resume emphasis: model evaluation, offline/online metrics, ranking/search, LLM/product eval, data quality, error analysis, Python ML.
Interview prep: model metrics, ranking metrics, eval design, A/B tests for ML products, data quality, debugging model regressions.

### Track E: Ads / Measurement / Marketing Science DS
Target companies: Google Ads/YouTube, Pinterest Ads, Apple Ads, Meta Ads, Snap Ads, DoorDash Merchant Ads, Stripe/ OpenAI Marketing.
Resume emphasis: incrementality, attribution, MMM/MTA, advertiser ROI, auctions/bidding if relevant, causal inference.
Interview prep: ads marketplace metrics, causal measurement, lift studies, holdouts, confounding, dashboards/storytelling.

### Track F: Strategic / Finance / GTM / Ops DS
Target companies: OpenAI Strategic Finance, Stripe Growth/GTM, Salesforce, Google Business DS, Uber People/Insurance, AppleCare.
Resume emphasis: forecasting, segmentation, unit economics, executive dashboards, business decisions, operational reliability.
Interview prep: ambiguous business cases, forecasting, cohort analysis, dashboard design, stakeholder prioritization.

## Recommended First Move

Create four resume variants first:

1. Product DS / Experimentation
2. Marketplace & Pricing DS
3. Payments / Risk / Fraud DS
4. ML Evaluation / Applied AI DS

For Yihan's current Goldman Quant Strats background, the highest-probability starting lane is not generic "Product DS" alone. The strongest wedge is:

Payments/Risk/FinTech DS + Marketplace/Pricing DS, then expand into Product DS once the resume has stronger product-language translation.
