## Scope And Filters

This list is for the first resume variant only: payment risk strategy / fraud analytics / risk decision science / marketplace integrity analytics. I intentionally separate it from the second variant, which will target fraud ML / real-time model deployment / feature pipelines.

Hard filters:

- Location must be United States.
- California is best; other US locations or US remote are acceptable.
- Strong JD fit with this resume: risk metrics, fraud/risk analytics, decisioning, backtesting, experimentation, monitoring, false-positive/friction tradeoffs, product/risk strategy.
- Seniority is now explicit:
  - Best: NG / Associate / 0-2 years / "all levels" where leveling can fit early-career.
  - Good: 3-5 years.
  - Lower priority: 5+ years, Staff, Principal, or heavy senior ownership.
- Compensation preference:
  - Big tech / known fintech can be kept even if pay is not ideal.
  - Smaller companies should ideally clear roughly $130k base or be unusually strong domain matches.

## Seniority Key

- `S0`: NG / associate / 0-2 years / likely early-career friendly.
- `S1`: 3-5 years, realistic stretch.
- `S2`: 5+ years or senior, possible but lower probability.
- `S3`: Staff/principal/very senior, mostly referral/stretch or JD-language reference.
