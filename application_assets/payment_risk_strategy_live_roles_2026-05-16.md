## Scope

This list is intentionally narrow. It includes roles that are strongly aligned with the first resume variant:

- Payment risk strategy
- Fraud/risk analytics
- Risk decision science
- Risk monitoring / oversight
- Risk metrics, KRIs/KPIs, dashboards, backtesting, experimentation, rollout monitoring
- Loss vs false-positive / friction / approval / conversion tradeoffs

It excludes or deprioritizes roles that are primarily fraud ML engineering, model-serving, deep learning, feature-store, or production model ownership. Those belong to the second resume variant: `payment_fraud_ml_resume`.
